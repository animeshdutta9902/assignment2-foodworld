Idli

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • Salt to taste
  • Water

Steps

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind them to a fine paste and mix with salt and water to make a batter.
  3. Ferment the batter overnight.
  4. Steam the batter in idli molds for 10-12 minutes.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with ginger-garlic paste and salt.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Add the marinated chicken and cook until done.
  5. Stir in cream and garam masala, cook for another 5 minutes.

Chole (Chickpea Curry)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp chole masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Pressure cook the soaked chickpeas with salt until soft.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes, cook until mushy.
  4. Add chole masala and cooked chickpeas, simmer for 10-15 minutes.

Samosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 2 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix flour, ghee, and salt to make a dough, set aside.
  2. Heat oil, add potatoes, peas, and spices, cook for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Roll dough into small circles, fill with potato mixture, and fold into triangles.
  4. Deep fry until golden brown.

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Mix yogurt, spices, and salt to make a marinade.
  2. Marinate paneer cubes for at least 1 hour.
  3. Skewer the paneer and grill until charred.
  4. Brush with oil and serve hot.

Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 3 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind them to a fine paste and mix with salt to make a batter. Ferment overnight.
  3. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and onions, sauté until golden.
  4. Add potatoes and spices, cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Spread batter on a hot griddle to make a thin crepe, fill with potato mixture, and fold.

Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Saffron strands
  • Oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Cook rice with saffron and set aside.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt and biryani masala.
  3. Heat oil, sauté onions until golden, add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add marinated chicken, cook until done.
  5. Layer rice and chicken in a pot, cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.

Rajma (Red Kidney Bean Curry)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups red kidney beans (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp rajma masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Pressure cook the soaked kidney beans with salt until soft.
  2. Heat oil, sauté onions until golden, add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes, cook until mushy.
  3. Add rajma masala and cooked kidney beans, simmer for 10-15 minutes.

Palak Paneer

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 2 cups spinach, blanched and pureed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil, sauté onions until golden, add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes, cook until soft.
  2. Add spinach puree and garam masala, cook for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add paneer cubes, simmer for another 5 minutes.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup khoya
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix khoya, flour, and baking soda to make a dough.
  2. Form small balls from the dough.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling water and sugar until sticky.
  4. Deep fry the dough balls until golden brown and soak them in sugar syrup.
